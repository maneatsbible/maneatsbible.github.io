title: Lots of Grace
date: 2026-02-16
---

The burden of the word of the LORD to the churches, which came to Christopher, a brother in Jesus Christ and a plain Bible preacher of Him.

The churches possess an abundance of parking lots which they hardly use, keeping these spaces empty at night, forcing homeless Christians, the least among our brothers and sisters, to sleep in dangerous places. This situation is a shame on those men who call themselves pastors under Jesus Christ, the Good Shepherd who said the last shall be first, and the first shall be last, in the kingdom of God.
